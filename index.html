Join the only Game Jam hosted at 1700m.

What is it?

The Hüttenjam 2019 is a game jam, where people gather in chalets and work on game project for intense 48 hours.
The Hüttenjam 2019 is a joint venture of the Game Development Graz Association and the Alpen-Adria Universität Klagenfurt.
It takes place from Sep 26 to Sep 29 on the beautiful Turracher Höhe and provides a unique opportunity to work with professional game developers in teams.

Pricing and Services

Registration fee is 100€ and covers lodging in one of the chalets (we booked four) in a double room. Of course one can use the sauna there too.
What we cannot provide
Food, drinks and snacks are not covered, but we hope to find sponsors to at least provide minimum supplies like pasta and muesli. Transportation has to be organized (partially) by the participants as public transport is sparse. While an internet connection is provided, it’s not the fastest, so make sure you have everything installed and for emergencies a mobile internet connection.

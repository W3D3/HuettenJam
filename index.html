Join the only Game Jam hosted at 1,700m.

What is it?

The Hüttenjam is a game jam, where people gather in chalets and work on games for intense 48 hours.
The Hüttenjam is a joint venture of the Game Development Graz Association and the University of Klagenfurt.
It takes place from April 13th to April 16th 2023 on the beautiful Turracher Höhe and provides a unique opportunity to work with professional game developers in teams.

Pricing and Services

The registration fee covers lodging in one of five chalets in a double (150€) or single room (300€). Of course, you can use the sauna there too. We also provide the breakfast on Friday so you can relax and socialize before getting to work.
What we cannot provide
Food, drinks and snacks are not fully covered, we only provide minimum supplies like ramen, pasta, and fruits. So, please bring your own drinks and food. Transportation must be organized by the participants as public transport is sparse. While an internet connection is provided, it’s not the fastest. Make sure you have everything installed and at best bring your own WiFi.
